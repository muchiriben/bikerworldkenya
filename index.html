Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see your question here, feel free to contact us.

For our beginner courses, we provide motorcycles and riding gear for your use during training. For intermediate and advanced courses, we recommend bringing your own motorcycle to train on the bike you'll be riding day-to-day. If that's not possible, we have a selection of bikes available for an additional rental fee.

Our beginner course has a 98% first-time pass rate. We attribute this to our small class sizes and personalized instruction. If you don't pass on your first attempt, we offer a free retake of the evaluation portion of the course.

For all courses, you must wear: a DOT-approved helmet (we have loaners available), eye protection, full-fingered gloves, long pants, a long-sleeved shirt or jacket, and over-the-ankle footwear. We strongly recommend motorcycle-specific protective gear, especially for advanced courses.

Upon successful completion of any of our Courses, you'll receive a completion certificate.

We train in light rain as it provides valuable real-world experience. In case of severe weather, we may reschedule the riding portion. If this happens, we'll work with you to find a convenient time to complete your training at no additional cost.
